About Propaganda

WE ARE A TRENDY HIP BRAND

Is a local brand that specializes in quality timeless streetwear, fashion accessories, and garments. We offer quality products at affordable prices.

The Story Behind the Brand

It started when Comfort began sketching clothing designs as a dream. Encouraged by his aunt, Comfort, along with his friends Njabulo and Siyabonga, started Propaganda to create a local brand that reflects their vision and creativity.

Vision

Propaganda's vision is to open an official physical store in Pretoria, expand nationwide, and establish an online store to provide convenience to our customers.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire people to pursue their dreams and embrace our slogan: "Have Vision Not Television."
